## XSL, XPath et XQuery

### QT4† CG (AKA the “XQuery and XSLT Extensions Community Group”)

The group aims to agree extensions to the XSLT 3.0 Recommendation published on 8 June 2017, along with supporting changes to the other specifications (XPath, Functions and Operators) on which it depends. 

A preliminary proposal describing requirements for such extensions can be found in Michael Kay’s Proposal for XSLT 4.0 published in the [Proceedings of XML Prague 2020](https://archive.xmlprague.cz/2020/files/xmlprague-2020-proceedings.pdf#page=121)

The abbreviation “QT” is short for “Query” and “Transformation” and was the short hand that the XQuery and XSLT Working Groups used to refer to the combined groups where coordination was necessary.

https://qt4cg.org
