#### Showyourwork!

[Showyourwork!](https://show-your.work)

>**Showyourwork!**, a workflow management tool for open source scientific articles. If you want to make your research article **reproducible**, **extensible**, **transparent**, or just downright **awesome**, you’ve found the right tool. **showyourwork!** automates your entire workflow, packaging it into a self-contained recipe that anyone can follow and—at the click of a button—use to reproduce your results.

### Julia

#### DrWatson

[DrWatson](https://juliadynamics.github.io/DrWatson.jl/stable/)

> DrWatson is a **scientific project assistant** software. It helps people manage their scientific projects (or any project for that matter).
>
> Specifically, it is a Julia package created to help people increase the consistency of their scientific projects, navigate them and share them faster and easier, manage scripts, existing simulations as well as project source code. DrWatson helps establishing reproducibility, and in general it makes managing a scientific project a simple job.

Cf. Datseris, George, Jonas Isensee, Sebastian Pech, et Tamás Gál. « DrWatson: The Perfect Sidekick for Your Scientific Inquiries ». *Journal of Open Source Software* 5, no 54 (2020): 2673. https://doi.org/10.21105/joss.02673.
